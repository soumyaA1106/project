Yadadri Sri Laxmi Narasimha Swamy

It is a popular Hindu Temple devoted to Lord Narasimha Swamy, an incarnation of Lord Vishnu..

ABOUT TEMPLE

Yadagirigutta is the most unique, beautiful and pleasant Hillock with
moderate Climate in all seasons and the temple is located at a distance of
about 60 KM from the Hyderabad, capital city of Telangana. The flow of
devotees / pilgrims visiting the temple for worship is very high since it is
situated near the capital city.
